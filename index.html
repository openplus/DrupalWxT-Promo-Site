About Drupal WxT

A little information about Drupal WxT to get you started.

Drupal WxT(Web Experience Toolkit) is a production proven platform with includes ready-made, re-usable tools and components for building and maintaining innovative enterprise sites that are multilingual, accessible, mobile and interoperable.

Drupal WxT is a non-proprietary distribution of Drupal, with best practice modules and methodology all wrapped up in one ready to go solution. It not only uses components that help with Cross-Browser compatibility, but components that are responsive and interchangeable within your layout.

While still complying with Web Accessibility and Web Usability standards, WxT makes use of advanced technology such as CSS3, HTML5, and jQuery in order to meet today's web functionality and reliability requirements.

With an ever growing community of support building modules, widgets and plug-ins. Drupal WxT is great for both internal collaborative intranets and public websites looking to adopt to the fast pace of the web while maintaining security standards.

Built by the community in collaboration with the Gouvernement of Canada

Benefits, to name a few

Just some of the reasons why this is a game changer.

  • Secure & Open
  • Extendable, Engagement & Feature ready
  • Transportable & Tested

Security

Security enhanced and Shared Services Canada vetted.

Modules and guidelines have been added to Drupal WxT to increase security, which has already been reviewed and approved for TRA (Threat Risk Assessment) by Shared Services Canada.

Open

Largest Open Source Community and contributors on the planet.

With over a million people behind Drupal.org, critical mass adoption is an understatement. Over 2x the size of the Linux developer community, we are vibrant local and international community with a public road-map and a public issue tracker. Even Drupal WxT has its own Issue Queue (with over 1200 resolved issues, and no active pending) on Github.

Extendable

Extendable with anything

Without "for profit" decision-making... Drupal is organically transparent and has vested interest to work with others, so it does. It supports Web Services integration and even CMIS (Content Management Interoperability Service) to integrate seamlessly with other Repositories.

Engagement

User Engagement Ready

Leave static pages in the dust and be ready for the user engagement features that help reduce GoC call center loads. Chat, Commenting/Discussions, Content ratings, Blogs, Follow content, Social Sharing and much more.

Feature ready

Feature Server ready

Drupal incorporates a "Features" distribution and App delivery service to help control versioned distributions and enhance sites. (similar to your iPhone Apps). Add a Drupal WxT GoC approved Blog, and other controlled features easily.

Transportable

Transportable, Versatile & Innovation

There is no licensing gauntlet with Drupal, making it agile and easy to evolve and move. You always share the Intellectual property, allowing you to harness and motivate developers to be collaborative and innovative. Making GoC more agile and keeping up with the pace of the web.

Testing

Extensive GoC testing.

There has been extensive GoC testing including automated Continuous Integration. Selenium Unity scripted cross-browser and multi-device testing is an automated process.
